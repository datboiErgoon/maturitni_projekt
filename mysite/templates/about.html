Idea

The aim of the work was to create a social web application that would focus
exclusively on working with MP3 files and their ID3 metadata. At the same
time, I also wanted a users who could log in to the website and be able to interact
with other users in some way and give feedback.

Project

The result of the project is a functional social web application for creating music playlists. The user can log in to the website and then upload their music creations in the form of posts. Other visitors can listen to and rate the playlist. Also, users can follow each other and thus have better access to new playlists.
